Prediction for July 20

After analyzing 10 years of data from July 20 in Sandusky, Ohio, we observed a consistent pattern of moderately high temperatures ranging between 82°F and 87°F, with frequent midday humidity and occasional rainstorms. Based on this, we predict the following for July 20, 2025:

  • High Temperature: 85°F
  • Low Temperature: 62°F
  • Chance of Rain: 50%
  • Humidity: 75%
  • Wind: SW at 10 mph
  • Lake Water Temp: Around 74°F

These numbers reflect recurring historical trends, especially the spike in warm, humid air during mid-July.
